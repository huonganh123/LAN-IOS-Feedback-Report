📌 PM Summary

During the CBT period, testers submitted ~150 crash reports and ~45 screenshot feedback items. The overall experience was positively received in terms of content and graphics, but technical stability is the biggest launch risk. The most frequently recurring issues are, in order: (1) resource download / update failures and crashes when entering the game, (2) crashes when sharing to Instagram, (3) lag / screen freezes, (4) character / graphics display issues (mosaic, missing visuals, black screen), (5) audio not playing (voice / BGM), (6) translation quality and EN dubbing quality, and (7) UI alignment issues on iPad.

🔍 Details by Issue Group

Each item includes a description, representative tester quotes, and suggested directions for Dev.

P0 1. Resource download / update errors → freezes & crashes

This is the largest complaint group. Testers reported interrupted resource downloads, getting stuck at 98–99.99%, having to repeatedly spam the "confirm" button to continue, excessive download size (displaying ~600MB but downloading beyond that), and the "repair" function forcing a full re-download, followed by freezes or crashes.
"After nearly 20 minutes of spamming the confirm button due to a resource download error... I only managed to download 2/6.7GB before the app froze and suddenly exited."
"got to 99.99% verifying resources, then crashed"
"After repair, it forced me to re-download all resources."
💡 Suggested fix / optimization: Review the download/patch and resource verification flow: add download resume, remove repeated manual confirm requirements, fix size calculation logic (excess downloads), and avoid repair = full re-download (download only missing parts). Consider SEA-proximate CDN and resource compression.
📱 Devices reported: iPhone 15 Plus, iPhone XS, iPhone 12, iPad (9th gen), iPhone X...

P0 2. Random crashes / freezes during play & app switching

The game exits while reading the story, when switching to another app and returning, or randomly crashes after around 15–30 minutes of play. Some devices freeze completely and require force-closing from multitasking before relaunching.
"This game crashes and hangs easily, once I open it around 15-20 minutes, it tends to crash and stop responding"
"crashed when switching between apps"
"The app exited while I was reading story 6-1."
💡 Suggested fix / optimization: Profile memory leaks and lifecycle management (background/foreground). Check for memory leaks during long sessions; handle state saving/restoration when the app enters the background.
📱 Devices reported: iPad (9th gen), iPhone 16 Pro, iPhone 15, iPhone X, iPhone 13...

P0 3. Crash when tapping "Share" to Instagram

A very clear and reproducible pattern: when testers receive a new card / complete daily tasks and tap to share via Instagram, the game suddenly closes (on some devices, a speaker distortion sound is heard before the crash).
"I just want to share the gacha to Instagram then it crashed"
"When I tapped share via Instagram, the speaker crackled once and then the game crashed."
"The game crashed while I was sharing an in-game image (daily task)."
💡 Suggested fix / optimization: Fix the share/Instagram SDK flow: check image/bitmap handling before sharing, add fallback when Instagram is not installed, and inspect audio session issues when opening the share sheet. This bug is easy to reproduce → should be prioritized.
📱 Devices reported: iPhone 13, iPhone 14 Pro Max, iPhone 11, iPhone XS Max, iPhone 15...

P0 4. Character & graphics display issues

Character visuals disappear / only the head appears without the body, character images become mosaic, black screens occur, zoom-in cut-scene details turn blank, home screen shaders break, and story progression gets stuck.
"The main story screen is either black or only shows a head."
"Almost all story scenes are mosaic, and the home screen is also mosaic."
"The game does not display characters, and dialogue does not appear automatically."
💡 Suggested fix / optimization: Check the render/shader pipeline and character asset loading by resolution; handle missing assets by showing placeholders instead of blank screens/floating heads. This is strongly related to the resource download issue (#1).
📱 Devices reported: iPhone 13 Pro, iPhone 13, iPhone XR, iPad Air 2, iPhone 8 Plus...

P0 5. Server overload / unable to enter the game

Many reports mention "unable to enter the game", "network anomaly", and "server crashed", especially during peak hours. Testers warned that day-one experience may involve waiting and downloading for several hours.
"Tried to log in but game says network anomaly, tried to repair but it just crashed instead"
"服务器崩溃了？进不去啊"
"现在卡的进都进不去。说我次数不足"
💡 Suggested fix / optimization: Load test infrastructure and login queue for launch day; improve network error messages so they are more user-friendly than just "network anomaly"; implement automatic retry handling.
📱 Devices reported: iPad mini (A17 Pro), iPad (9th gen), iPhone 15 Pro, iPhone 11 Pro Max...

P1 6. Audio: voice & BGM not playing

Many testers could not hear character voice and/or BGM even when enabled, or the BGM stopped by itself; some reported the voice becoming "robotic" after an update; voice does not auto-play and requires tapping "review". Some devices continue playing music even after exiting the game.
"Since logging into the game until now, I have never heard character voice or background music."
"the voice went robotic"
"Many times, I can still hear the music after exiting the game."
💡 Suggested fix / optimization: Review AudioSession management (iOS): initialize/release correctly, handle interruptions when entering background, and sync voice with dialogue progression (auto-play). Check audio decoding issues that cause "robotic" voice after patching.
📱 Devices reported: iPhone 16 Pro Max, iPad (8th gen), iPhone 11, iPad (9th gen)...

P1 7. Lag / frame stuttering

Lag/stuttering occurs when entering/exiting the game, increases during longer play sessions, and is especially noticeable in rhythm stages (music mini-games).
"The longer I play, the more frequently the game stutters."
"The rhythm stages are quite choppy."
"It is really very laggy, and the game keeps crashing."
💡 Suggested fix / optimization: Optimize FPS & GC; especially profile rhythm stages (timing is frame-sensitive). Optimize for older devices (iPhone 8/X/11).
📱 Devices reported: iPhone 11 Pro, iPad Pro 11", iPhone 13, iPhone 15...

P1 8. Localization quality & EN dubbing

This is a notable qualitative feedback group (from Screenshots). Issues include incorrect/shifted meanings, typos, inconsistent translation (skirt/dress), inconsistent proper names (Cody vs pinyin, "Charlie Su"), Chinese text mixed in, and EN voice not matching text. EN dubbing is criticized (reading "cough cough cough" aloud, unnatural acting).
"text and English voice do not match"
"Zha Lisu name... incorrectly translated as Charlie Su"
"The actor should not read it as Cough, cough, cough"
💡 Suggested fix / optimization: Hire/review translation and localization QA with native speakers (EN/CN, VI, TH); standardize proper names in the glossary; check that EN voice matches the script; fix the specific typo/format issues testers pointed out in screenshots.
📱 Devices reported: iPhone 16 Pro Max, iPad (A16), iPhone 17 Pro, iPad Air...

P2 9. UI misalignment / edge overflow on iPad

On iPad, many elements (buttons, text, episode titles) are misaligned or overflow beyond the screen edge; buttons are too close to the edge; spacing between reward and replay buttons is too tight and causes mis-taps.
"The text is off screen on my ipad"
"Too close to the edge, fix the position of the button pls"
"increase the spacing between the rewards... and the replay buttons"
💡 Suggested fix / optimization: Check responsive layout for iPad aspect ratios and safe areas; increase touch-target spacing for buttons that are easy to mistap.
📱 Devices reported: iPad Pro 11" (3rd gen), iPad (A16), iPhone 16 Pro Max...

P2 10. Gacha & game balance

Testers reported receiving a 6★ card while the character was not in the banner / not owned; requested a preferred LI selection mechanism; combat difficulty is too high and discouraging (cannot clear even with auto/manual at the required level); exit/ad buttons block drag-and-drop interactions.
"I rolled Te Tu Le 6stars at the first time, but when I check he is not exists in banner"
"please make the battle part not too hard for the player? it demotivates me"
💡 Suggested fix / optimization: Review gacha logic and banner display (a received 6★ must appear in the inventory); rebalance stage difficulty; review the placement of ad/exit widgets that block interactions.
📱 Devices reported: iPhone 16 Pro Max, iPhone 8...

P3 11. Feature requests (polish)

Positive suggestions: 1x–1.25x–1.5x–2x reading/viewing speed; a hide-text button to view character illustrations (like Tears of Themis); improve subtitle clarity & font settings; add tone marks to character-name pinyin; change the "inputting" UI in messages to "…".
"Allow to increase speed of watching e.g. 1x, 1.25x, 1.5x, 2x"
"a button that could give us the ability to hide the text to view only the illustration"
💡 Suggested fix / optimization: Add to post-launch backlog: reading speed, illustration-viewing mode, subtitle/font customization, and pinyin tone marks. This is a good engagement signal from testers.
📱 Devices reported: Multiple devices
